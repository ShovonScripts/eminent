Privacy Policy

At Eminent Overseas & Consultants, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our overseas education consulting services. This includes:

Personal Information

  • Full name, date of birth, and contact details
  • Email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Passport details and national identification information
  • Emergency contact information

Academic Information

  • Educational qualifications, certificates, and transcripts
  • Language proficiency test scores (IELTS, JLPT, etc.)
  • Academic references and recommendation letters
  • Statement of purpose and study plans

Financial Information

  • Bank statements and financial documents
  • Sponsorship information and income certificates
  • Tax documents and financial statements

We only collect information that is necessary for providing our services. You have the right to know what information we collect and how it is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for legitimate business purposes related to our educational consulting services:

Purpose Information Used
Educational Counseling Academic background, interests, and goals
University Applications Academic records, test scores, personal statements
Visa Processing Support Personal details, financial documents, passport information
Service Communication Contact information for updates and notifications
Compliance & Legal Requirements Information required by educational institutions and immigration authorities

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on:

  1. Contractual necessity to provide requested services
  2. Legal obligations and compliance requirements
  3. Legitimate interests in providing educational consulting services
  4. Your consent for specific processing activities

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share your information only when necessary and with appropriate safeguards:

With Educational Institutions

We share your academic information with universities, colleges, and language schools in Japan and UK for admission purposes only.

With Government Authorities

We may share information with immigration authorities, embassies, and consulates as required for visa processing and compliance.

With Service Providers

  • IT service providers for system maintenance
  • Document translation and verification services
  • Secure document storage and management services

We never sell your personal information to third parties. All data sharing is done with proper confidentiality agreements and data protection measures.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

Technical Security Measures

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers with firewall protection
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

Organizational Security Measures

  • Staff training on data protection and privacy
  • Confidentiality agreements with all employees
  • Clear data handling policies and procedures
  • Regular privacy compliance audits

Physical Security Measures

Our office premises are secured with access controls, surveillance systems, and secure document storage facilities.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary:

Retention Periods

  • Active client data: Retained for 5 years after service completion
  • Inquiry data: Retained for 2 years after last contact
  • Financial records: Retained for 7 years for tax compliance
  • Legal documents: Retained as required by applicable laws

Data Deletion

Upon expiration of retention periods, we securely delete or anonymize your personal information using industry-standard methods.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right Description
Access Request a copy of your personal information
Correction Request correction of inaccurate information
Deletion Request deletion of your information in certain circumstances
Restriction Request restriction of processing in certain cases
Objection Object to processing based on legitimate interests

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 10. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic:

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language and theme preferences
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site

Cookie Management

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

We do not use cookies for advertising purposes or to track you across other websites.
