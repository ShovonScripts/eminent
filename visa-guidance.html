Japan Student Visa

Japan Student Visa Requirements

Comprehensive guidance for Japan student visa applications. We help prepare accurate documentation for language schools, technical colleges, and universities.

Academic Documents

  • All academic certificates & transcripts
  • School/college leaving certificates
  • Japanese language proficiency certificates (N5/N4)
  • Acceptance letter from Japanese institution
  • JLPT/NAT test results (if applicable)

Financial Documents

  • Bank statements (6 months minimum)
  • Sponsor's financial documents
  • Income certificates of sponsor
  • Tax returns (if applicable)
  • Proof of relationship with sponsor

Personal Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 2 years)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Birth certificate
  • National ID card copy
  • Medical certificate (if required)

Important Notes for Japan Visa

  • Japanese language proficiency is mandatory for most study programs
  • Financial documents must show sufficient funds for tuition and living expenses
  • Documents must be translated into Japanese by certified translators
  • Processing time: Typically 2-4 weeks after document submission
  • No work permit during first 3 months for language school students

UK Student Visa

UK Student Visa (Tier 4) Requirements

Comprehensive support for UK student visa applications. Guidance for university admissions and proper documentation for UK immigration.

Academic & English Requirements

  • CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies)
  • IELTS/TOEFL English test results
  • All academic certificates & transcripts
  • University offer letter
  • References (academic & professional)

Financial Requirements

  • Bank statements (28 days minimum)
  • Proof of tuition fee payment
  • Sponsor's financial documents
  • Loan sanction letter (if applicable)
  • Financial sponsor declaration

Personal & Health Requirements

  • Valid passport (with blank pages)
  • Tuberculosis test certificate
  • ATAS certificate (for sensitive subjects)
  • Birth certificate
  • Police clearance certificate

UK Visa Application Process

1

CAS & Document Collection

Guidance on obtaining CAS from university. Assistance with collecting and verifying all required documents for UKVI.

2

Online Application

Assistance with completing online visa application forms. Guidance on accurate information entry and document uploads.

3

Biometric Appointment

Guidance on booking and attending biometric appointments. Preparation for visa application center procedures.

4

Interview Preparation

Mock interview sessions for credibility interviews. Focus on academic intent and financial capability questions.

Important Notes for UK Visa

  • Financial maintenance requirement: £1,334 per month (for London)
  • IELTS requirement: Minimum 6.0 overall for most universities
  • Healthcare surcharge: £470 per year (must be paid before application)
  • Processing time: Standard 3 weeks, priority 5 working days
  • Work permission: 20 hours/week during term time
